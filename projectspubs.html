Publications/Papers

Aging disrupts spatiotemporal regulation of germline stem cells and niche integrity

Time-lapse Microscopy, Fluorescent Microscopy, Wet-Lab, MATLAB


In this publication, I contributed to the identification of a decline C. elegans' germline fecundity in early adulthood, linked to disrupted Notch signaling and age-related changes in the DTC/niche. I created and analyzed DTC data and visualizations using MATLAB, focusing on active RNA transcription, and utilized fluorescent and live imaging microscopes to support my work.


The Magnetic Harmonic Oscillator

Python, SciPy/Pandas/Matplotlib, circuit design, electronic testing





This exploration into fundamental electromagnetic laws aims to support expected theoretical results of electromagnetic induction and utilizes a magnetic harmonic oscillator and large coil to do so. We designed the physical experiment from our derived theoretical basis. We collected extensive data and utilized adaptive fit optimization and predictive modeling techniques to assess our data with theoretical results.


Research beyond STEM

The Japanese Impact on Vietnamese Revolutionism, 1905-1945

This research examines how Japanese colonial influence shaped Vietnamese nationalism and revolution from 1905 to 1945, focusing on the changing views of key figures and the impact on the August Revolution of 1945.

Transforming Grief into Art: Ravel's Commemorative Journey in Le Tombeau de Couperin

This research examines Maurice Ravel's Le Tombeau de Couperin (1914-1917), exploring its blend of Baroque influences and French Impressionism. It analyzes the themes of commemoration and memory, focusing on how Ravel’s stylistic and technical choices reflect post-World War I sentiment and personal tragedy.
