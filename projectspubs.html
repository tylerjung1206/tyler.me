Publications/Papers

Aging disrupts spatiotemporal regulation of germline stem cells and niche integrity

Time-lapse Microscopy, Fluorescent Microscopy, Wet-Lab, MATLAB


In this publication, I contributed to the identification of a decline C. elegans' germline fecundity in early adulthood, linked to disrupted Notch signaling and age-related changes in the DTC/niche. I created and analyzed DTC data and visualizations using MATLAB, focusing on active RNA transcription, and utilized fluorescent and live imaging microscopes to support my work.


The Magnetic Harmonic Oscillator

Python, SciPy/Pandas/Matplotlib, Circuit Design, Electronic testing


This exploration into fundamental electromagnetic laws aims to support theoretical results of electromagnetic induction and utilizes a magnetic harmonic oscillator and large coil to do so. We designed the physical experiment from our derived theoretical basis, collected extensive data, and utilized adaptive fit optimization and predictive modeling techniques to assess our data with theoretical results.


Calorimetric Efficiency of the Thermoelectric Peltier Device

Python, SciPy/Pandas/Matplotlib, Electronic testing


This experimental study investigates the Peltier effect, where electromotive forces induce temperature gradients in thermoelectric devices. We designed and executed precise measurements of temperature and electrical parameters to evaluate device efficiency relative to theoretical thermodynamic models. Data analysis using curve fitting and predictive techniques validated performance and highlighted key loss mechanisms impacting efficiency.
